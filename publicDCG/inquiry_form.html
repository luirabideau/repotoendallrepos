Ad Hoc Query Submission

If your search needs are not covered by our current database features, submit an ad hoc query here. These requests will be sent to our team for processing. All inquiries for ad hoc search queries should be directed to erabidea@hawaii.edu.

General Material Request

The collections are housed in closed stacks on the 5th floor of Hamilton Library. Materials can be requested online at any time through this form and are generally available 15 minutes after the request.
